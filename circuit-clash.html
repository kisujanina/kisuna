About Circuit Clash 1.0

Circuit Clash 1.0 is the flagship national robotics and technology festival organized by BUP Robotics Club. This groundbreaking event brings together the brightest minds from universities across Bangladesh to compete, innovate, and showcase their technological prowess across six thrilling segments.

6 Competition Segments

Multiple categories to showcase your skills

160K BDT Prize Pool

Lucrative rewards for winners

500+ Participants Expected

From top universities nationwide

Industry Mentors

Learn from leading tech professionals

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Circuit Clash 1.0?

Any currently enrolled university student in Bangladesh can participate. Valid university ID is required for registration.

Can a team register for multiple segments?

Yes! Teams can register for multiple segments. However, separate registration fees apply for each segment.

What should we bring on the event day?

Bring your university ID, registration confirmation, and any equipment needed for your segment (robots, laptops, etc.). Detailed guidelines will be emailed to registered teams.

Is accommodation provided?

Limited accommodation may be available for teams from outside Dhaka. Please mention this in your registration form for consideration.

What if we need to cancel our registration?

Cancellations before November 1st will receive 50% refund. No refunds after November 1st. Contact us immediately if you need to cancel.
