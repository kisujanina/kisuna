About Us

One of the most prestigious clubs of BUP

The BUP Robotics Club at Bangladesh University of Professionals is a student-led innovation hub dedicated to pushing the boundaries of robotics and AI technology. Founded by passionate ICT undergraduates, we foster a culture of creative problem-solving through hands-on projects, competitions, and collaborative research.
